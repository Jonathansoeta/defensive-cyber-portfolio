# A10 – Discover privacy technique used offline

## What I did
I looked for privacy techniques used offline in everyday life.

## Examples I found
1. Privacy screen protector on a phone or laptop
2. Shredding paper documents
3. Locking paper documents in a drawer
4. Covering personal details on printed forms
5. Keeping ID cards and bank cards in a wallet

## What I learned
Offline privacy is still important because personal information can be exposed even without the internet.

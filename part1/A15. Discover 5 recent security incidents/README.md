## Recent security incidents
1. Alleged Terror Plot Targeting Key Sites (March 2026)
- A 20-year-old man was charged with planning a mass-casualty terrorist attack.
- Targets allegedly included police headquarters, WA Parliament, and places of worship.
- Authorities found weapons, ammunition, and detailed plans.
- Investigated jointly by WA Police, AFP, and ASIO.

2. Fuel Theft Hit-and-Run Injuring Worker (March 2026)
- At a petrol station in Armadale, a driver fled without paying and ran over an attendant.
- The victim suffered injuries and was hospitalized.
- The suspect remains at large, raising concerns about violent opportunistic crime.

3. Invasion Day Rally Bomb Attempt (Jan 2026)
- A man threw a homemade explosive device into a crowd of ~2,500 people in the CBD.
- The device failed to detonate but could have caused mass casualties.
- Treated as a potential terrorist attack by authorities.

4. Large-Scale Criminal Cash Smuggling at Perth Airport (2025–2026 outcomes)
- AFP seized over $3.2 million in suspected criminal cash from travellers in 2025.
- One offender was jailed in January 2026 after being caught with illicit funds and drugs.
- Highlights ongoing organised crime and money laundering risks.

5. Cyclone Narelle Security & Emergency Threat (March 2026)
